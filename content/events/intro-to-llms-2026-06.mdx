## What to expect

In this session we'll walk through the core ideas behind large language models (LLMs) — the technology behind tools like ChatGPT, Claude, and Gemini.

We'll cover:

- **What is a language model?** — the intuition behind predicting the next token
- **Tokens and embeddings** — how text becomes numbers
- **The Transformer architecture** — a plain-English explanation of attention
- **How LLMs are trained** — pre-training, fine-tuning, and RLHF
- **Hands-on demo** — playing with a small model in the browser

## Who should come?

Anyone curious about AI, regardless of background. We'll assume you know what a neural network is in vague terms, but nothing more.

## Bring

Your laptop if you have one — we'll do some interactive exercises via a web interface, no setup required.
